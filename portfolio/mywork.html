Michigan State University | Migrant Student Services

MSU's Migrant Student Services has a program called CAMP (College Assistant Migrant Program). These students in the program typically come from Southern states and are of low income. I worked with the programs directors to develop a plan and discuss how to address some issues. An issue I decided to focus on was their lack of winter clothing. I designed a flyer to let them know what to wear and what not to wear, accompanied with an inexpensive location to get these items.
